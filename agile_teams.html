Facilitate better planning

Make Overflow an integral part of your sprint cycle and enhance your stories with a powerful UX artifact.

Embed user story maps, iteration trackers, or any other documentation made with Overflow in Trello, JIRA, Confluence, or any other tool used for backlog refinement, sprint planning, and sprint review.

Encourage feedback

Upload your presentation to the Overflow Cloud to get a share link that you can send to your peers. Installing the Overflow app is not essential to view the link’s content.

Your team can view your design presentation on any popular device, such as desktops, phones, and tablets.

Everyone with the link can provide feedback through Overflow comments, facilitating quicker iteration and lean decision-making in any collocated or remote team.

Enhance transparency

Overflow documents can be stored in private, fully, or partially shared folders, depending on your team’s structure and needs..

Team members can download the underlying documents of all share links they are given access to.
